Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does Scaler collect any data?
    No. Scaler does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All calculations run locally on your device.
  • Which scales are supported?
    Scaler supports architectural scales such as 1:20, 1:50, 1:100, 1:200, 1:500, 1:1000 and a custom scale.
  • How do I report a problem?
    Please send an email with a description, your device model, iOS version, and screenshots if possible.
